Tathmeer International

We Lead the Fabric Industry
Overview

Tathmeer International is a leading company in the export and import of high-quality scout clothing, military clothing, and camping supplies. Established in the 1980s in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, by Dr. Abdullah Alshebl, the company has built a solid reputation through decades of expertise in fabrics, materials, and innovative sewing methods. With a history of catering to both the public and private sectors, Tathmeer International has grown into a key player in the regional and international markets.

Our Legacy
Historical Background

The company initially began its operations by importing scout and military clothing for the public sector. Recognizing the growing demand, Tathmeer expanded its portfolio in the 1990s to include camping supplies, tapping into the outdoor recreational market. The company’s success in this field led to further growth, allowing it to diversify its customer base across both public and private sectors.

As demand for high-quality products grew, Tathmeer International ventured into manufacturing, with its own clothing factory now in the final stages of completion. The factory is expected to further enhance the company's ability to meet local and international needs with greater efficiency and precision.

What We Can Do
Core Competencies

Expertise in Fabrics & Sewing: Over the years, Tathmeer International has established itself as a "house of expertise" in fabrics, materials, and sewing techniques, ensuring the highest standards of quality in all its offerings.

Global Sourcing: The company partners with major factories in China, Bangladesh, Vietnam, and Pakistan, as well as local suppliers in Saudi Arabia, to ensure a steady and reliable supply of products tailored to client needs.

Custom Solutions: From military-grade uniforms to outdoor camping gear, Tathmeer provides a wide range of customized solutions for clients in various sectors, ensuring that all products are durable, comfortable, and fit-for-purpose.

And Beyond!
Products & Services

Aspirations and Ambitions
Future Plans

With the impending launch of its new clothing factory, Tathmeer International is set to expand its production capabilities. The factory will focus on producing high-quality clothing for local and international markets, strengthening the company’s competitive edge and reducing lead times for customers. The company also plans to explore new product lines in the outdoor adventure sector, enhancing its already robust range of camping supplies.
