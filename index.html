About the Founder – Dr. Abdullah Al-Shabel

Dr. Abdullah Alshebel, in the 1980s. the visionary founder of Tathmeer International, was born in Buraidah, Al-Qassim. He completed his early education in Al-Qassim before pursuing higher studies in the United States. He earned a Bachelor of Science degree from Kent State University (KSU) in Ohio, followed by a Master’s in Mathematics from the University of Denver, and a Ph.D. in Teaching Mathematics from the University of Northern Colorado.

Upon returning to Riyadh, Dr. Al-Shebel dedicated himself to academia, teaching at the Teachers’ Colleges under the Ministry of Education. His entrepreneurial journey began with importing camping equipment, which soon evolved into a specialized business in scout supplies and military clothing. He founded Tathmeer International, transforming it into a leading supplier for government projects, including being the approved provider of scout supplies for the Ministry of Education.

With over 37 years of expertise in fabrics and manufacturing, Dr. Al-Shabel has built a reputation for excellence, sourcing materials from top Asian suppliers in countries like China, India, Bangladesh, and Pakistan. Despite Tathmeer’s gradual growth, Dr. Al-Shabel remains committed to humanitarian work and actively supports non-profit initiatives, ensuring his business reflects his values of quality, integrity, and community service.
